Year Made: 2020

My Role: Programmer and Ui/Ux Designer

Github For Project

Made during the third year of my education. The biggest project i've worked on yet. Also the first time that im not just a programmer on the projects and im taking on multiple roles. Spies and soldiers is a Multiplayer Social deduction game remminicent of among us where most players are good soldiers but some are evil spies that wants too secretly dispose of the soldiers

Most fun to work on?

In our game the good guys (Soldiers) has too complete a number of tasks (Minigames) too win before the spy can kill everyone. It was my job too create these task. It was really fun too quickly get as many off these out as possible as we needed alot of them in the level. I made around 15 minigames and a few off these has different variants of themself.
