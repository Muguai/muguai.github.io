Learn Chinese Game

Development Time: Less than a Week

My Role: Worked Alone

This game is a typing of the dead style game where you have to drag the right word to to the right symbol before they can get too the middle and hurt you. This was a project i worked on alone during my Game based learning course. I did not have that much time too work on this project but i suprised myself by having a pretty fine game only after a few days of work

What was most the fun too work on?

For me the most fun too work on was actually the Menu Screen believe it or not. I made a system in which you could easily add your own Chinese Characters too practice during each game loop. It was fun too make the game easily customizable and i think it added a lot too the game.
